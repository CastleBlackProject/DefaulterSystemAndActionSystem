Defaulter System

Intro

This is a mini project made for keeping track of attendance of students in colleges for different subjects and for teachers to ease the process of maintaining attendance records for students

Use

Students can use this to check and maintain their attendance records for different subjects. Teachers can use this to maintain the huge attendance data digitally easily accessible to all the students thereby reducing their workload burden

Features

1. This project has various modules for every requirement of attendance management
2. There are features to create edit and display various modules such as staff info ,student info ,attendance and even subjects
3.This project helps u keep an entire track of various information about students , staff , subjects in each year in every sem and much more

Database Tables Needed

This project is modular in nature hence we have used multiple tables to keep the data organised in the database some tables used are
1. student_master
2. staff_master
3. subject_master
4.branch_master
5. semester_master
6. year_master
7. academic_session_master
